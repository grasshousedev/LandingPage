Privacy Policy

We take your privacy very seriously. This policy describes all information collected or submitted on the Twizzle application, and what we do with it.

Questions

If you have questions about deleting or correcting your personal data please contact us at contact@twizzle.app

Gathering of Personally-Identifying Information

Twizzle allows you to access the direct-message functionality provided by Twitter. We act as a web browser and do not collect any personally-identifying information.

When you use Twitter functionalities, like direct messages, to which Twizzle gives you access, you trust Twitter with your information and accept their terms of use, which can be found here: https://twitter.com/en/tos.

Protection of Certain Personally-Identifying Information

No Personally-Identifying Information is transmitted to our servers when using Twizzle.

We collect anonymous usage data and crash reports through Sentry. This makes it easier for us to troubleshoot problems and improve Twizzle. Hence Usage Data is collected only to operate and improve the app and customer support.

If you opt in to receive our newsletter when you buy the app, your email will be stored in the Mailgun service. If you prefer not to receive any communication from us, you can unsubscribe at any time by clicking “Unsubscribe” in our emails, or by contacting us at contact@twizzle.app

Security

The security of your personal data also depends on you. You should not share your Twitter username and password with anyone. If you believe or find out that your login credentials are in the wrong hands, you should immediately change your password. We also recommend activating Twitter 2-Step Verification. We are not responsible for such unauthorized access.

By using Twizzle and providing your information, you authorize us to collect, use, and store your information outside of the European Union.

Any future change to this policy will be posted to this page. Your continued use of the Twizzle app after we make any of these changes is deemed to be in acceptance of those changes, so please check this page periodically for updates.

Your Consent
